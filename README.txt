Reyam AI website
================

This version includes your photo and updated Reyam AI branding.

How to update the live website on Vercel:
1. Unzip this file.
2. Upload the folder named "reyam-ai-site" to Vercel as a new deployment.
3. Vercel will update the live project.

Easy edits:
- Main website text: index.html
- Colours/design: styles.css
- Contact form email: script.js
- Your photo: images/reyam.jpg

Important edits before launching publicly:
1. In script.js, replace your-email@example.com with your real email.
2. In index.html, replace https://calendly.com/your-link with your Calendly link.
3. Update prices if needed.
